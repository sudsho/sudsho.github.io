Research Focus

My dissertation, "Hierarchical Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning for Quality-Aware Intelligent Distributed Manufacturing," proposes a three-level AI framework validated with real-world data from a leading computer server manufacturer (IBM):

  • Level 1 — Product-Level Defect Analytics: Causal discovery using Counterfactual Multi-Agent RL (CF-MARL-SMV2D) and cost-sensitive defect disposition prediction with label reconstruction under incomplete annotations.
  • Level 2 — Factory-Level Scheduling: Lexicographic Hierarchical Actor-Critic (LHAC) deep RL for adaptive server-to-cell scheduling that maximizes throughput while minimizing tardiness under uncertainty.
  • Level 3 — System-Level Coordination: Hierarchical Multi-Agent RL (HMARL) for order offloading and inventory transshipment across distributed manufacturing sites.
